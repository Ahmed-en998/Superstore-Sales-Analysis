# 📊 Superstore Sales Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes a retail Superstore dataset to evaluate business performance, identify profitability issues, and provide actionable recommendations.

The goal is to simulate a real-world freelance data analysis project using SQL and business insights.

---

## 🛠 Tools Used
- SQL (MySQL Workbench)
- Excel (for initial exploration)

---

## 📂 Dataset
- Source: Superstore dataset
- Records: 9,694 order line items
- Time Period: 2014 - 2017

---

## 🔍 Key Business Questions
- Which regions are most profitable?
- Which categories and products are underperforming?
- How do discounts impact profitability?
- What are the main drivers of profit and loss?

---

## 📊 Key Insights

### 🟢 Business Performance
- The business is stable with profit margins improving from ~10% to ~13%.
- However, performance has plateaued, indicating room for optimization.

### 🌍 Regional Analysis
- West region is the top performer (highest sales & profit).
- Central region underperforms with the lowest profit margin.

### 📦 Product Analysis
- Technology is the most profitable category.
- Furniture is underperforming, especially:
  - Tables (major losses)
  - Bookcases

### 💸 Discount Analysis
- Discounts above 20% lead to losses.
- Discounts above 30% result in severe negative margins (-48%).
- Many sales occur without discounts, proving discounts are not always necessary.

---

## ⚠️ Business Problems
- Heavy losses in specific sub-categories (Tables, Bookcases)
- Inefficient discount strategy
- Weak profitability in certain regions (Central)

---

## ✅ Recommendations

### 🔴 High Priority
- Reduce discounts above 20%, especially in Furniture
- Investigate cost/pricing issues in Tables

### 🟡 Growth Opportunities
- Invest more in Technology (Copiers, Phones)
- Expand successful strategies from West region

### 🟢 Optimization
- Use targeted discounts instead of uniform discounts
- Apply discounts strategically based on product margins

---

## 📌 Conclusion
This analysis highlights key profitability challenges and growth opportunities. By optimizing discount strategies and focusing on high-margin products, the business can significantly improve its overall performance.
